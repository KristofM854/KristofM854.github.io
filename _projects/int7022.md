Coordination of the IAEA Technical Cooperation Project INT7022, "Strengthening Ocean Health for Sustainable Development: A Global Approach using Nuclear and Isotopic Techniques." Working with Member States across the globe to strengthen marine biotoxin monitoring capabilities, support harmonised analytical protocols, and contribute to food safety regulation.

The project includes scientific trainings, expert missions, interlaboratory comparisons, and work on reference material access across 20+ participating countries.
