Kristof Moeller

I help organisations monitor, understand, and respond to harmful algal blooms — through research, data tools, and policy-ready science.

Currently based at the IAEA Marine Environment Laboratories in Monaco, I work at the intersection of marine science, environmental monitoring, and public health. My research focuses on harmful algal blooms, marine biotoxins, and the analytical and data systems that support food safety and policy response — combining fieldwork, LC-MS/MS, and data analysis in R.

I am available for scientific consulting, expert advisory contributions, and speaking engagements on harmful algal blooms, marine biotoxins, and environmental data analysis.
